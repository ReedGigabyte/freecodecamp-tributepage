Elle woods standing in a courtoom, wearing her signature pink

Elle Woods is a fictional character from the movie Legally Blonde (2001). Originally a fashion major from California, she decides to follow her ex-boyfriend to Harvard Law to prove she's 'serious' enough to be with him. Harvard intially proves to be a disaster, but through hard work, authenticity, and the support of her new community, Elle proves her detractors wrong and saves an inoccent woman from life in prison.

Elle's story eventually recieves a sequal in Legally Blonde 2: Red, White, and Blonde, and a reimagining in Legally Blonde: The Musical
